CONNECTED PLAY GUIDE

How to play

You are building a kill corridor that protects your portal while your heroes and champions wear the wave down. The basic flow is simple:

  1. Place the portal. This is what the enemies want to destroy, so the rest of your setup is built around protecting it.
  2. Place 9 barriers. Use them to force enemies through a long path instead of giving them a straight line to the portal.
  3. Place your Warrior. She is the bottleneck. Enemies cannot pass through her, so she is your wall and frontline anchor.
  4. Select your free relic. Take the relic that best fits the run you want to build.
  5. Place your damage and healing heroes. Enemies pass through these heroes. They do not block the path, and enemies do not attack them directly, so position them where they can work safely behind your Warrior.
  6. Check for Champion NFTs. If Champion NFTs are found on DFK Chain or Metis, you will get an option to select one for the run.

Here's how champions change the game

  • Champions are special power picks that can push a run much further than a normal lineup.
  • If you own eligible Champion NFTs on DFK Chain or Metis, the game will let you choose one before the run starts.
  • They do not replace the normal setup steps. They add to them, so you still need a strong path, a good Warrior hold, and support behind it.
  • A well-timed champion can stabilize a rough run or help you break through harder waves that a normal hero setup would struggle with.

Important run mechanics

  • Last Chance gives you a retry on the last wave instead of ending the run immediately. Use it to recover from a bad placement, a weak timing window, or a rough wave spike.
  • Hiring additional heroes becomes important as the run grows. Build your core first, then add more damage, healing, and utility when the game gives you the chance to hire and expand the board.
  • Your pathing matters more than raw damage. A clean barrier path plus a strong Warrior choke is what gives the rest of your team time to do their job.
